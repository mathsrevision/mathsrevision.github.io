What is mathsrevision.github.io?

This is a website made for free and hosted for free that aims to give college students maths revision for free. If you couldn't tell already, we're all about doing things for free.  

mathsrevision.github.io provides college students with free revision for multiple topics, including algebra, geometry, sequences and series, trigonometry, calculus, statistics, mechanics and much more.

Why use mathsrevision.github.io?

This website simply provides a wealth of knowledge about a vast range of mathematical ideas and, more importantly, how to use them to pass your exams. Unlike other sources, we will not over complicate things by providing knowledge that is not needed to pass your exams, such as the history of the concepts etc. Instead, we prefer a more quote-on-quote, "Get to the point", approach.

The three principles of our revision

Simplicity.

We're not going to complicate you on topics that don't need complicating - on this website, concepts will be explained efficiently in a way that anyone could understand, whether they be basic or advanced.

To the point.

The website has multiple sections, with some sections containing long, in-depth revision and some sections containing quick revision notes - this way, we can always live up to our slogan of "Any time, any place".

Easy to use.

Have you ever been on a website and gotten frustrated with its navigation? I know we have. Luckily for you, our website is easy to navigate, with clearly laid out sections that have a purpose for existing that makes sense.
