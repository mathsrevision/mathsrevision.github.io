Quick Notes: Algebra & Functions

Quick notes on the topic of Algebra & Functions in mathematics.

1. The Laws of Indices.

There are six laws of indices that you must know during your exams:

Law One: $n^{0}=1$

Law Two: $n^{a}n^{b}=n^{a+b}$

Law Three: $(n^{a})^{b}=n^{ab}$

Law Four: $\frac{n^{a}}{n^{b}}=n^{a-b}$

Law Five: $n^{-a}=\frac{1}{n^{a}}$

Law Six: $n^{\frac{a}{b}}=(\sqrt[b]{n})^{a}$
